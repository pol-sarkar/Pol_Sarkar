About Me

I am a Plant Molecular Biologist focused on understanding how plant pathogens evolve, spread, and interact with their hosts and vectors at the genomic level.

My work combines viral genomics, nanopore sequencing, and bioinformatics to improve the detection and assembly of plant-associated pathogens, particularly in complex field and vector-derived samples.

By integrating wet-lab and computational approaches, I aim to contribute to more accurate diagnostics and better strategies for managing emerging plant diseases.
