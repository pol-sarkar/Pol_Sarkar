About Me

I am a Plant Molecular Biologist focused on understanding how plant pathogens evolve, spread, and interact with their hosts and vectors at the genomic level.

My work combines viral genomics, nanopore sequencing, and bioinformatics to improve pathogen detection and genome assembly.

I aim to contribute to better diagnostics and management strategies for emerging plant diseases.
